Short bio:

I joined Lodders in November 2014. I am head of the Town and Country Homes team, as well as a partner within the Real Estate and Agriculture and Estates teams. Prior to joining Lodders, I worked for a large regional firm in the South East.

I advise clients on and specialise in a wide range of property aspects relating to landlord and tenant, investment property portfolios, agricultural property and charity and educational property. My clients tend to be business owners, property investors, charities with property portfolios, and agricultural land owners.

I haven’t always worked in the law, prior to qualifying a as solicitor, I worked in the USA and France and in the educational sector in England as a bursar.
